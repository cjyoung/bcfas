Art League Kid's Stuff


The Brazosport Art League provides multiple and varied programs for children.
School classes may schedule tours of the Gallery. Artist comment on art, it's creation and forms. The students may make pencil sketches of the examples from the current show with supplies provided by the League. Contact the Center for scheduling.
