Harry Sargent Portfolio



Harry Sargent is a retired Dow Engineer who began oil painting in Doris Munson’s class at Brazosport College in 1969. His production of art increased after retirement in 2001. He has been active in the Brazosport Art League in various positions. He enjoys art inspired by his occasional vacations and faces. He paints in oils and sketches in pencil and pen.
