AUDITION INFORMATION

February 24 @ 2:30 p.m., 25 & 26 @ 7 p.m. • Auditions and crew signup for the April 19 – 28 production of Leading Ladies by Ken Ludwig • This energetic comedy of mistaken identities features two down-on-their-luck Shakespearean actors who have been reduced to performing in the Amish countryside of Pennsylvania. THEATER WORKSHOPS

January 23 & 30 • 6:30 - 9:30 p.m. • Audition Workshop • Win That Role! Workshop participants will practice “cold-reading" auditions and will be taught strategies to discover character and conflict with little or no preparation. Local actor, director, and theatre educator, Wes Copeland, will lead this two-session workshop designed for adult actors new to the stage, as well as those with years of experience. Registration is limited to 25 people, 17 years old and above. Registration fee includes both sessions, and participants are strongly urged to attend both sessions. Online registration at brazosportcenterstages.org only – $25 for two sessions/non-BCS Members; $15/BCS Members. This is the first in a series of adult education workshops underwritten by the BCS Audience Development & Education Outreach fund.

February 20 & 27 • 6:30 - 9:30 p.m. • Director’s Workshop • This workshop is designed to help both experienced and would-be directors understand the fundamentals of directing a play. Basic script analysis, working with designers, stage composition, movement, and the “language of actors” are covered and practiced over this two-session course. Registration is limited to 25 people, 17 years old and above. Registration fee includes both sessions, and participants are strongly urged to attend both sessions. Online registration at brazosportcenterstages.org only: $25 for two sessions/non-BCS Members; $15/BCS Members. BRAZOSPORT PLANETARIUM

 In the Planetarium Lobby: Space exploration wall with NASA TV, interactive computer stations and displays • ViewSpace: Images from Hubble, Spitzer & Chandra telescopes with the latest news about our solar system, updated daily by astronomers at the Space Telescope Science Institute in Maryland. • Gift shop items include the latest in astronomy books, supplies & gifts for all ages. Lobby is open to the public at no charge while school is in session, Tuesday – Friday, 10 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. and before Planetarium Shows.

PLANETARIUM SHOWS

January 1, 8, 15, 18 & 29 and Friday, January 25 • 7 p.m. • Cosmic Colors • Cosmic Colors will take you on a wondrous journey across the entire electromagnetic spectrum. Discover the many reasons for color – like why the sky is blue and why Mars is red. Take a tour within a plant leaf and journey inside the human eye. Investigate x-rays by voyaging to a monstrous black hole and then back at your doctor’s office. You will even see the actual color of a dinosaur – based on recent evidence. Get ready for an amazing adventure under a rainbow of cosmic light! Also, Tonight’s Sky • What’s over your house tonight? Tickets available 20 minutes before the show. $5 Adults, $3 Students. Call 979–265–3376 for information.

February 5, 12, 19, 22 & 26 and Friday, February 22 • 7 p.m. • Winter's Bright Circle • Gemini, Orion, Taurus, Auriga, Canis Major… Bright stars, ancient tales, interesting astronomy and the planet. Also, Tonight’s Sky • What’s over your house tonight? Tickets available 20 minutes before the show. $5 Adults, $3 Students. Call 979–265–3376 for information.

BRAZOSPORT MUSEUM OF NATURAL SCIENCE

 Rated one of the ten best museums in Texas by Frommer’s Travel Guide. Largest collection of shells on display in the South with shells from around the world. Life–size dinosaur casts; amazing wildlife; exciting fossils; glamorous gems & minerals; fascinating archeology; hands–on children's area piques children’s interest in the natural world. Displays change frequently. Visit www.bmns.org or call 979–265–7831 for more information. Open Tuesday–Saturday • 10 a.m.–4 p.m. and Sunday • 2–5 p.m. and during Center Stages productions. Free admission.

January 13 • 2 – 4 p.m. • BMNS Annual Meeting • Trustees and members gather to hear about everything that happened in 2012. The public is very welcome and there is no admission charge. After a very brief intermission, the first BMNS Board of Trustees meeting will be held as well to elect officers for 2013.

MUSEUM AFFILIATES

February 5 • 7 p.m. • Sea Shell Searchers of Brazoria County • Meetings, the 1st Tuesday of every month in the Museum, are open to the public. Bring a covered dish to share. For more information, see www.seashellsearchers.org

January 8 & February 12 • 7:30 p.m. • Brazosport Archaeological Society • Meetings, in the Museum, are open to the public. Join them in their study of the prehistory and history of the lower Brazos River region. The Society is a non-profit organization created to stimulate an active interest in the discovery, conservation, and salvage of archaeological sites, and the recording and preservation of archaeological remains using scientific procedures. For more information, visit www.bmns.org.

January 28 & February 25 • 7 p.m. • Brazosport Birders and Naturalists • Meetings, the 4th Monday of every month in the Museum, are open to the public. All those interested in birding and conservation of our natural resources are welcome. For more information, visit their page on Facebook.
