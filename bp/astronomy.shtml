Brazosport Astronomy Club


We are a group of amateur astronomers dedicated to the study and enjoyment of the night sky! Anyone interested is welcome to attend meetings on the 3rd Tuesday of each month following the Planetarium show. No dues. No telescope needed. Astronomy Club member outreach includes school star gazes and other events to encourage education and enjoyment of astronomy!
