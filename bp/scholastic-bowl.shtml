Scholastic Bowl


Offers a friendly competition between teams of four adult and/or high school. Teams answer quests on subjects such as science, the arts, history, geography, current events and local activities. Fundraiser benefits science education for the Brazosport Planetarium and The Center or the Arts & Sciences.
