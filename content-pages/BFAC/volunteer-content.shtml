The Center for Arts & Sciences and it's Member Groups rely on the effort of community volunteers. Here are a few examples of volunteer opportunities.

Brazosport Art League


Gallery Sitters
  • No art expertise is needed
  • Volunteer for a morning or afternoon shift, once a month
  • Greet guests
  • Make sales from the Art Shop or main Gallery exhibits
    (Training is provided)
  • Enjoy periodic luncheons held to thank Gallery volunteers
  • Gallery sitters recieve a free ticket for sitting in the Gallery prior to curtain and during intermission for BCS productions


Call the Gallery at 979-265-7971 for more information. The person who answers the phone is one of our awesome volunteers!

Brazosport Museum of Natural Science


Museum Sitters
  • No science expertise is needed
  • Volunteer for a morning or afternoon shift, once a month
  • Greet guests
  • Make sales from the Museum Store (Training is provided)
  • Museum sitters recieve a free ticket for sitting in the Museum prior to curtain and during intermission for BCS productions


Please call the Museum at 979-265-7831 for more information. The person who answers the phone is also one of our awesome volunteers.

Brazosport Center Stages



Center Stages productions are staffed entirely by volunteers who direct, produce, design and build sets, design and install lighting, design and construct costumes, serve as ushers and serve intermission refreshments.

Volunteers sign-up during auditions for each production of the season.

Check out the Brazosport Center Stages website for more information on upcoming plays, auditions, and other volunteer opportunities or call 979-265-7661


Brazosport Planetarium


Planetarium volunteers assist Planetarium Director Judi James by:
  • Staffing planetarium shows
  • Working on educational displays
  • Helping with special planetarium functions and events such as public star gazing


For more information call Planetarium Director Judi James at 979-265-3376
