Leading Ladies


Opening April 19


Production Dates
April 19, 20, 26 & 27 @ 8 p.m.
April 21 & 28 @ 2:30 p.m


This energetic comedy of mistaken identities features two down-on-their-luck Shakespearean actors who have been reduced to performing in the Amish countryside of Pennsylvania. When they learn that a wealthy old woman in search of her two long-lost heirs is on her deathbed, they devise a plan that sends them on the performance of their lives. Performed in the Arena.

Educational Workshop



Theater Dance Essentials


May 4 • 10 a.m. - 12 p.m.

4th Grade - High School

May 11 • 10 a.m. - 12 p.m.

High School Students & Adults

High School Students that have dance experience can attend the May 11 session

$10/members and $20/non-members


This class will teach some basic dance steps utilizing the floor space, discuss audition process, give short background on theatre dance, talk about the importance of proper posture and stance & review stage directions, along with the instructions of how to use props.

Students are required to bring proper dance shoes.
No flip flops, open-toed shoes, heels, etc.
Tennis shoes are okay, but character shoes, jazz or ballet shoes, or flats are recommended.
