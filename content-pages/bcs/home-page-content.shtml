The Diary of Anne Frank


Adapted by Wendy Kesselman • Arena Theater
In this gripping new adaptation from the original stage play by Goodrich and Hackett, newly discovered writings from the diary of Anne Frank, as well as survivor accounts, are interwoven to create a contemporary impassioned story of the lives of people persecuted under Nazi rule. Craig Fritz, director.

Everything You Never Knew You Never Knew About Directing

Learn the foundations of directing for the stage.

Brazosport Center Stages presents this workshop to equip directors and actors with the basic tools to stage your vibrant, original interpretation of a play.
