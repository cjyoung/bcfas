Leading Ladies


Opening April 19


Production Dates
April 19, 20, 26 & 27 @ 8 p.m.
April 21 & 28 @ 2:30 p.m


This energetic comedy of mistaken identities features two down-on-their-luck Shakespearean actors who have been reduced to performing in the Amish countryside of Pennsylvania. When they learn that a wealthy old woman in search of her two long-lost heirs is on her deathbed, they devise a plan that sends them on the performance of their lives. Performed in the Arena.

Tickets are $13/adults; $10/children
