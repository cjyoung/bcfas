Leading Ladies


Auditions and Crew Signup • April 19 – 28


Production Dates
February 24 @ 2:30 p.m., 25 & 26 @ 7 p.m.

Production of Leading Ladies by Ken Ludwig • This energetic comedy of mistaken identities features two down-on-their-luck Shakespearean actors who have been reduced to performing in the Amish countryside of Pennsylvania. When they learn that a wealthy old woman in search of her two long-lost heirs is on her deathbed, they devise a plan that sends them on the performance of their lives. Cast includes 4 women and 5 men, ages 18 and up. Director: Dennis Ulrich. Performed in the Arena Theater.





A Perfect Ganesh


Production Dates
May 3, 4 @ 8 p.m. & May 5 @ 2:30 p.m.

A Reader’s Theater production of Terrence McNally’s poignant portrayal of two middle-aged lady friends who throw themselves into a rousing tour of India. Each one harbors her own secret dreams of what the fabled land of intoxicating opposites will do for the suffering she hides within. The elephant-god Ganesha is the spiritual center around which the play spins itself, drawing upon the tragic and the comic, until a breathtaking release arrives for both women at his hands. A special bonus production, not included in BCS subscription tickets.
Tickets are available at the door.
