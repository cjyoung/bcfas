For older students, 12-17, Gurrard Teen guild provides a 2 week intensive drama workshop culminating in two performances of this year's production: Snow White and the Seven Dwarves

Over two weeks, teens audition, build sets, gather and make props and costumes, learn basic theatrical make–up, hang lights, and learn parts to perform on stage.

Performing Shakespeare's Midsummer Night's Dream.
Workshop is June 10 - 22. Monday thru Saturday 9 am - 4:30 pm
Bring sack lunch, jacket, 3 ring binder, and wear work clothes and closed toe shoes

Performances will be Friday June 21 @ 5pm and Saturday June 22 @ 2:30 pm
Admission $5 OR $3 with canned goods for the Food Pantry
Children 6 and under FREE

Though Teen Guild meets throughout the year (usually the third sunday of the month) you do not need to be an active participant to join us for the summer workshop. Class size is limited to 30.
