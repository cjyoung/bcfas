TBD • 2014 Scholastic Bowl • Fundraiser held in the large theater supporting science education at the Planetarium and many programs offered at The Center. Teams of up to four people put their heads together to answer questions on subjects such as science, the arts, history, geography, current events and local activities. We encourage community organizations, alumni groups, church groups, family members and work groups to compete. Admission is $5 at the door. The entry fee for each team is $100 and money is available for high school teams that wish to compete, but do not have the entry fee. For more information, call Sarah Heller at 979-798-1966.
