Volunteer with the Symphony




Do you have a dusty instrument sitting in your closet just begging you to play a little tune?
Come be a part of the Brazosport Symphony Orchestra.

What do I need to do?

All you need to do is come to one of our rehearsals listed here to either listen or join in on rehearsals. We just want to get to know you and would love to meet you.

Do I need to Audition?

No longer do you need to audition, we have left that process in the past. Just come to rehearsals and introduce yourself is all you need to do.

Is there an age requierment?

We welcome everyone to our symphony from high school students to those in there 90s, no age restrictions here.

What if I don't play an instrument?

We have other opportunities for you to be involved with the Symphony. There is our Brazosport Symphony League that helps support the symphony and it's players.

Do I need to be a member?

Yes, you do need to join the Brazosport Symphony League. Contact the League for more information.
