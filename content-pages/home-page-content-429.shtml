Brazosport Center Stages Presents


BONUS PRODUCTION

A Perfect Ganesh

A Reader's Theater Production

Production Dates
May 3 & 4 at 8:00 p.m.
May 5 at 2:30 p.m.


A Reader’s Theater production of Terrence McNally’s poignant portrayal of two middle-aged lady friends who throw themselves into a rousing tour of India. Each one harbors her own secret dreams of what the fabled land of intoxicating opposites will do for the suffering she hides within. The elephant-god Ganesha is the spiritual center around which the play spins itself, drawing upon the tragic and the comic, until a breathtaking release arrives for both women at his hands. A special bonus production, not included in BCS subscription tickets.
