Brazosport Center Stages Presents



Win that Role! Audition Workshop

January 23 & 30, 6:30p-9:30p


Registration Fee
$25 Non-Members
$15 Members
Registration fee includes both sessions, and participants are strongly urged to attend both sessions.

Are you tired of quaking in your boots each and every time auditions roll around? Find yourself stuck in a rut, cast in the same types of roles over and over?



Brazosport Center Stages presents this workshop to equip participants with techniques necessary to stroll into auditions with confidence and ace the dreaded “cold reading” time after time!

Registration is limited to those 17 years old and above. This is the first in a series of adult education workshops underwritten by the BCS Audience Development & Education Outreach fund.
