Anne Frank emerges from history as a living, lyrical, intensely gifted girl confronting the increasing horror of her time with honesty, wit, and determination. An impassioned drama about the lives of 8 people hiding from the Nazis in a concealed storage attic, The Diary of Anne Frank captures the claustrophobic realities of daily existence: their fear, hope, laughter, and grief. Throughout, however, Anne's voice shines through.
