Elizabethan Madrigal Feast Raffle Items


A king’s ransom worth of incredible treasures is available to lucky winners at the Madrigal Raffle. Visit the lobby of The Center to take a look.

Items are separated into two categories: Gold and Silver.
Raffle tickets for Gold items are $20 each and $10 each for Silver items.

Tickets can be purchased at The Center office 10 a.m. - 4 p.m. Monday - Friday.
Tickets will also be for sale prior to each performance, during intermission and, except for the last night, after each performance.
We accept Cash, Check, Master Card, Visa, and Discover
