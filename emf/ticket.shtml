The biennial Elizabethan Madrigal Feast is the major fundraiser supporting the $560,000 annual operating costs of The Center for the Arts and Sciences.

Tickets for the Elizabethan Madrigal Feast are $65/person for evening performances and $55/person for Sunday matinee performances. All tickets include a full dinner and performance.

Underwriters and Sponsors of the Elizabethan Madrigal Feast are given the earliest opportunity to purchase tickets. Most performances usually sell out, so select your seating as early as possible.
